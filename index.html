Welcome to Ninelgammon!

What exactly is "Ninelgammon"?

Well,to put it simply, its a site made by Ninel, on which you learn how to play backgammon , about the projects that the creator made involving Backgammon, and the actual game!

There already are some online Backgammon sites, why play here?

You don't have to! Even though i love the game i cannot negate the fact that it's the infamous "old people" game, mainly seeing this game be played by your grandpa , your elderly teachers , etc. That being said, i feel like this is the main reason that most of the websites that revolve around backgammon are really outdated, maybe not even have a secure connection,so i thought this project would be great for reviving the Backgammon fanbase and make the interface a bit more modern.

Is "Ninelgammon" different than regular Backgammon?

Not really,the only thing changed about the game other than the obvious name is that the doubling rule is not valid here, check the "How to play" section on the left.
